What is Cisco Live?

Cisco Live delivers education and inspiration to technology innovators worldwide through large-scale in-person and digital events, as well as on-demand education. It is the premier destination for Cisco customers and partners to gain knowledge and to build community. As the place where the best and the brightest minds in IT come to learn, Cisco Live will ignite creativity, deliver practical know-how, and accelerate the connections that fuel your digital future.


IT Leadership Program

Overview

Over the past few years, we’ve seen digital transformation accelerate far into the future. We are living in a new world where the workplace can truly be anywhere, where the needs of the workforce are diversifying, and where the work culture is shifting. How do we navigate this new normal? The IT Leadership Program at Cisco Live is designed to deliver the business acumen and technological expertise needed to help you and your organization succeed in this new world. Join us February 7-9 in Amsterdam for the 2023 IT Leadership Program to explore the most important shifts affecting IT, to make connections with industry peers, and get the information and inspiration needed to build the foundation for your digital future.

Explorer Program

The Explorer pass is your passport to discover Cisco innovations and technologies, meet with Cisco engineers, customers and partners and enjoy the opportunity to network with your peers. Even though Explorers cannot attend the technical or IT Leadership breakout sessions, there are many other ways for you to explore and learn at Cisco Live. Attend the Opening Keynote and hear from our Executive leadership team, attend a Product and Strategy Overview session or workshop in the DevNet Zone to learn more about the latest Cisco technologies, or connect with a Cisco expert in the Technical Solutions Clinics area.


Choose Your Experience

Are you interested in a world-class curriculum of technical education and training designed to keep your skills sharp amid ever-changing technology innovations? The Technical Education program is the one for you.

Are you focused on the business of IT and looking to explore the most important shifts affecting IT, to make connections with industry peers, and get the information and inspiration needed to build the foundation for your digital future? The IT Leadership program is the one for you.
